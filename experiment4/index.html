Experiment 04: Stairway to Heaven

Description

Inspired by the Terraria mod Wrath of the Gods
A central golden path extending horizontally around j = 0.
Pillars: A randomly colored pillar appears every 15 tiles and extends infinitely downwards
Sky: The stars twinkle slightly, and interactive stars appear when you click on individual tiles (other than stairs and pillars).

Technical

Path: If j ∈ {0,±1,±2}, draw a yellow diamond.
Stars: Draw a circle when XXH.h32("tile:"+[i,j], worldSeed) % 25 === 0. Use sin() and lerp() to adjust transparency and flickering frequency.

Reflection

For this experiment, I feel okay about it. It is fun and I learned a lot from it. Specifically, the hash seed, I took a while to understand how it works. But, I want to say three projects are a bit too much, because I don't have too many time to fix the flaws and add more interesting things to the project.
Something I like about p5.js is that there are a lot of examples on the internet. So it is easy to get what I want.
